It is said that the sea captures the heart of the soul both, wild and calm, mysterious and consoling, dangerous and beautiful; it has always made us feel some way throughout its metaphors and tempers.

Somewhere between the crashing waves and the towering boulders a spectacular scene arises, mesmerising every witness and holding him into a relaxing and meditative state. From a dazzling gleam on the horizon, you shall feel the warm embrace of the curved blue waves, revealing newness, cleanliness and the sense of purpose.
